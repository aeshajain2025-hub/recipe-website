Pancakes

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla essence.
  3. Gradually combine the wet and dry ingredients to form a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with butter.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter onto the pan to form a pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Repeat for remaining batter.
  8. Stack the pancakes and serve warm.

Tip: Serve with honey, maple syrup, or fresh fruits for extra taste.
