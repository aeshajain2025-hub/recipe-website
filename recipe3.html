Biryani

Preparation Time: 60 mins

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup chicken or mixed vegetables
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan and sauté whole spices until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add chicken/vegetables, yogurt, and spices. Cook until well combined.
  7. In another pot, boil rice until it is 70% cooked.
  8. Layer the cooked rice over the masala.
  9. Sprinkle coriander and mint leaves on top.
  10. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  11. Mix gently and serve hot with raita.
