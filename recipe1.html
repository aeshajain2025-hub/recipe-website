Pasta

Preparation Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Mixed vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook pasta until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add vegetables (if using) and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Add fresh cream and mix well to make it creamy.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and mix gently.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Sprinkle grated cheese on top and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious creamy pasta!
