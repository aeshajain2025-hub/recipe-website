Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add milk, butter (or oil), and vanilla essence. Mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into the greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Remove from oven and let it cool completely.
  9. Slice and serve your delicious homemade chocolate cake!
