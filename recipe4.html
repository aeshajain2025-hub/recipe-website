Sandwich

Preparation Time: 10 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons green chutney or mayonnaise
  • 1 boiled potato (sliced)
  • 1 small cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • Grated cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Spread butter and chutney/mayonnaise evenly on the bread slices.
  2. Place sliced potatoes, cucumber, tomato, and onion on one slice.
  3. Sprinkle salt, black pepper, and grated cheese on top.
  4. Cover with another bread slice to form a sandwich.
  5. Grill the sandwich in a sandwich maker or pan until golden and crispy.
  6. Cut into halves and serve hot with ketchup or chutney.

Tip: You can add paneer or corn for extra flavor.
